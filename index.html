About No More Bad Gift

Gift giving can be awkward – the giver never really knows what the receiver wants, and the receiver feels uncomfortable asking. No More Bad Gift bridges that gap by creating a platform where you can share what you love with your family and friends. The gift giver can choose from your list and send a thoughtful present that truly matters.

Create Your Wishlist

Easily compile a list of items you love, so your friends and family know exactly what you like.

Find the Perfect Gift

Gift givers can browse your wishlist and choose a thoughtful gift without the awkward questions.

Share and Celebrate

Enjoy a gifting experience where everyone feels happy – no more bad gift experiences!
